Privacy Policy

Effective: July 23, 2026 Last updated: July 23, 2026
Plain-language summary: Water Sort Puzzle does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All game data (progress, settings, achievements) is saved only on your device and never leaves it. We do not use ads, analytics, or any third-party tracking.

1. About this policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Water Sort Puzzle ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the App on your Android device. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

The App is developed and published by an independent developer. If you have questions, contact us at the address in Section 9.

2. Information we do not collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

🚫

Personal identifiers — no name, email address, phone number, date of birth, or any account credentials. The App has no sign-up or login.

🚫

Location data — the App never requests or accesses your device location.

🚫

Device identifiers — no IMEI, advertising ID, or any hardware identifier is read or transmitted.

🚫

Camera, microphone, or contacts — the App does not request or use any of these permissions.

🚫

Financial data — the App contains no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or any payment flow. No financial information is ever processed.

🚫

Usage analytics or crash reports — no analytics SDK (Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.) and no crash-reporting library is included. No behavioral or diagnostic data is sent to any server.

3. Data stored locally on your device

The App saves the following data only on your device using Android's Room database and DataStore libraries. This data never leaves your device.

Data Purpose Storage Transmitted?
Level progress & stars Track which levels are completed and star ratings earned Room database (on-device) Never
Move count & elapsed time Score calculation and display within a game session In-memory only (discarded on exit) Never
Achievement records Display unlocked achievements in-app Room database (on-device) Never
Language preference Display the App in your chosen language DataStore Preferences (on-device) Never
App settings (theme, sound) Restore your preferences between sessions DataStore Preferences (on-device) Never
Onboarding completion flag Skip the introduction screens after first launch DataStore Preferences (on-device) Never

All locally stored data is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App, or you can clear it at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Water Sort Puzzle → Clear Data.

4. Android permissions explained

Permission Why it is needed Data accessed
INTERNET Required by the Android OS networking stack. The App uses this only to detect whether a network interface is reachable and display the "No Internet" screen when offline. Connectivity status only
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Reads the active network connection state (connected / disconnected) so the App can show the offline screen. No network traffic or personal data is involved. Connection state only

Neither permission is used to send any data to any external server. No other permissions are declared in the App's manifest.

5. Third-party libraries

The App uses the following open-source Android libraries. None of them collect or transmit personal data from users of this App:

  • Jetpack Compose / Material 3 — Google's UI toolkit; renders screens on-device.
  • Hilt (Dagger) — dependency injection framework; no data collection.
  • Room — SQLite database wrapper; stores data locally only.
  • DataStore Preferences — key-value preference storage; local only.
  • Navigation Compose — in-app screen routing; no data collection.
  • Lottie Compose — renders bundled animation files; no network calls.
  • Core Splashscreen — displays the Android 12+ splash screen; no data collection.
  • Gson — JSON serialisation used internally; no data collection.

No advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, or social SDK is included.

6. Children's privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because the App collects no personal information whatsoever, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy. We do not knowingly collect any data from any user, child or adult.

7. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with any third party for any purpose. There is no data to share — the App does not transmit any information off your device.

8. Security

All data is stored on your device using Android's standard application sandbox. Only the App itself can access its own database and preference files. We do not operate any servers that receive or store user data.

9. Changes to this policy

If the App's data practices change in a future update, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
